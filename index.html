Malware

Digiral Issue.

Malicious software designed to infiltrate or damage a computer system without the user's consent. Malware includes viruses, worms, ransomware, spyware, and adware, often spread through email attachments, infected websites, or software vulnerabilities.

Phishing

Digital Issue.

A fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details, by disguising as a trustworthy entity in electronic communication. Phishing attacks commonly occur via email, instant messaging, or social media, aiming to deceive recipients into disclosing confidential information or downloading malware.

Data Breaches

Digital Issue.

Unauthorized access, disclosure, or acquisition of sensitive or confidential data stored in digital systems. Data breaches compromise the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of information, leading to potential financial losses, reputational damage, and legal consequences for affected individuals or organizations.
